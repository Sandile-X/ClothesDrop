From the Founder

Hi, I'm Mihlali — welcome to Clothes Drop.

I know the frustration of finding fire fits online or from international stores, then getting hit with crazy shipping, import fees, and four-week waits. So I started sourcing clothes for both men and women, so you don't have to stress. Trendy, quality pieces without the international headache — I'm here for that.

I'm based right here in Mthatha, so I understand local needs, local weather, and local budgets. I'm not a big faceless store — I'm your neighbour building something real.

"Style without stress, price without compromise, and a seller who actually listens. I'm your girl." — Mihlali Sikroba
